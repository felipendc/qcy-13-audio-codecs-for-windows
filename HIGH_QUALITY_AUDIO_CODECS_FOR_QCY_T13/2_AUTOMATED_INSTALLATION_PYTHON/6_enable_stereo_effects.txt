After installing the codecs...

Pair your Bluetooth Headset / Earbuds to Windows.

Once it's paired to Windows, right click on the "Start Menu" on the Taskbar
and select "Device Manager" and delete the following drivers in this exactly order:


1) QCY-T13 Avrcp Transport
2) Microsoft Bluetooth LE Enumerator
3) Bluetooth Device (RFCOMM Protocol TDI)
4) Microsoft Bluetooth Enumerator

Now, turn off and turn on the Windows Bluetooth.

Done!
